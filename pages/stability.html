Basic Stability

What is the Basic Stability program?

Basic Stability is a program that unconditionally covers someone’s basic needs like rent, utility bills, food, etc.

Uses of Basic Stability

The main function of basic stability is to remove the side issues in life so people can focus on main goals, tasks, or interests. Not only can people stand on their own two feet, but by taking the “how do I make money?” overhead out of the equation, people are empowered to directly pursue their passions that may once be thought of as pipe dreams.

Below are a list of all other functions that Basic Stability helps with:

  • A Space to Stand on Your Own Two Feet
  • An Environment for Enablement
  • A Space for Empowerment, Self Belief, and Self Confidence
  • A Space to Follow Passions and Interests
  • A Space to Redefine Yourself or Figure Yourself Out
  • A Space for Healing Injuries or Inner Issues
  • An Escape of Toxic Environments
  • A Space to Focus
  • A Space for Unlimited Tries to Succeed
  • A Space for Fault Tolerance
  • A Safety Net for Unplanned Circumstances
  • A Safety Net During Crises (like Covid)
  • A Safety Net to Stand on Your Own Two Feet
  • A Space to Grow Side Businesses Organically and Not in a Rush
  • A Space for Deep Learning
  • A Space for Emotional and Mood Stability and Recharge
  • A Space without the “how do I make money?” overhead in life’s decisions

Who should receive Basic Stability?

Basic Stability is inclusive to these people:

  • You are laid off
  • You have health injuries or short or long term disabilities but your company doesn't cover them
  • You are living paycheck to paycheck, barely getting by
  • You are retired but can't pay the bills
  • You have special disabilities
  • You are a caretaker of specially disabled
  • You are stuck in a job you hate
  • You have imposter syndrome or you constantly feel the need to “fake it til you make it”
  • You are in a life transition or want to go on a spiritual journey
  • You are a single stay-at-home mom
  • You are a dependent who is used to relying on others to survive
  • You are a burden to family or loved ones
  • You are stuck in a toxic environment
  • You are not in an environment of enablement
  • You have inner issues like trauma and you need space to focus on the healing process
  • You fell into the cracks of society and society has neglected your needs, you cannot stand on your own two feet, or you can stand but there are problems overlooked
  • You can no longer do your old job and need to redefine yourself
  • You have a side business, side project, side hustle, or side hobby that doesn’t make money initially that you want to focus on instead of your current main job
  • You want to do something that has irregular or hit-or-miss income like book writing or streaming
  • You are a misfit
  • You are lonely and depressed
  • You feel behind in life by society’s standards
  • You are someone who needs to re-acclimate to society (like a veteran)

Permanent Basic Stability

Permanent basic stability is for those whose interests are not motivated by money or for those who are unable to stand on their own two feet.

Examples of activities not motivated by money:

  • Community Service
  • Volunteering
  • Activism
  • Open Source Developer
  • Art
  • Music
  • Helper of Society
  • Help the Pay It Forward Club's cause itself

These are people who want to create joy to the world or create joy for themselves.

Examples of those who are unable to stand on their own two feet:

  • Retired elderly without a way to pay bills
  • Special disabilities

The Big Picture Problem of Society

Basic Stability directly addresses the Sinking Ship problem and promote a more compassionate and free society. See Our Statement to the World for context.

It is an all inclusive, one-size fits-all solution that has many different uses and addresses many different issues in society.
